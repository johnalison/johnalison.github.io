-----–— Forwarded message ----–— From: Krystalyn Kovacs <krystalyn@cmu.edu> Date: Fri, Oct 11, 2024 at 12:57 PM Subject: Student employment information To: Manfred Paulini <paulini@cmu.edu>

  1. Contact student employment with the job description for approval (career@andrew.cmu.edu) (but they ended up forwarding my email to adegeorg@andrew.cmu.edu) (also not sure if this step is technically necessary, but I did it and they were very helpful)
  2. Find an approved wage –> https://www.cmu.edu/my-workday-toolkit/quick-guides/restricted/student-pay-ranges.pdf
  3. Make handshake post (contact career@andrew.cmu.edu to get an employer account set up)
  4. Collect applications
  5. Interview students
  6. Email michelle waters with their employment information –> sets them up on workday
  7. Email the offer letter to the student, and CC michelle waters. include start date
  8. If the student is working with student data, they need to complete this: https://www.cmu.edu/career/documents/on-campus-employment/for-supervisors/FERPA-Statement-of-Understanding.pdf

Extra resources: https://www.cmu.edu/career/student-employment/for-supervisors/index.html https://www.cmu.edu/my-workday-toolkit/quick-guides/restricted/undergraduate-student-job-profiles.pdf
