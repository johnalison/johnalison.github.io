Spurious-Signal

Spurious signal arises when the misspecification of the background model can mimic a signal under the null hypothesis. How much is the concept of "spurious signal" used outside of particle physics ? Even with in particle physics, most analysis don't often check for it. Could this be important beyond HEP ?
