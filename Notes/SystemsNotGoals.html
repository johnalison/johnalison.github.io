Positioning vs Predicting:

  • Positioning: Setting yourself up to have several different options to better respond to future scenarios (Anti-fragile)
  • Predicting: Taking actions assuming a belief about what the future is going to be like.

Positioning is the better long-term stategy. Prediction can win out in the short-term if you happened to be right Should always look for Positiong over Predition. Build in optionality, treat optionality as an asset and loss of optionality as a cost.
